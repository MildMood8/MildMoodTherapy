Who We Are

At Mild Mood Therapy, you are more than a diagnosis or a “case.” You are a whole person with a story, identities, and experiences that deserve to be seen and respected.

The practice centers LGBTQIA+ affirmative care and welcomes people of all sexual orientations, gender identities, and expressions, as well as allies. Sessions are rooted in compassion, curiosity, and collaboration so you can explore what it means to live more authentically on your own terms.

We honor how culture, race, gender, sexuality, trauma, and systems shape mental health, and we work alongside you to move toward self-acceptance, empowerment, and sustainable change.

Bianca Carver, MS, LMHC

Licensed Mental Health Counselor

Bianca works primarily with adults navigating anxiety, depression, Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), sexuality, trauma, and racial or cultural identity concerns. She has a special interest and deep experience supporting LGBTQIA+ clients.

Her approach blends Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Solution-Focused Therapy (SFT), Motivational Interviewing, and a strongly affirming, collaborative style. Sessions are structured enough to feel held and flexible enough to meet you where you are each week.

Mission

Mild Mood Therapy exists to be a soft landing place in a world that often asks you to be smaller, quieter, or different than you are. The mission is simple:

  • Create a space where every part of you is allowed in the room.
  • Offer culturally aware, identity-affirming care that honors lived experience.
  • Support you in building a life that feels more aligned, grounded, and authentic.

You are invited to come exactly as you are and explore what healing, growth, and self-discovery can look like when you do not have to perform or shrink.
