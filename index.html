Emotional Health Looks Good On You!

Step into Mild Mood Therapy, a haven where diversity is celebrated and hearts have room to breathe. Based in Melbourne, Florida, the practice centers the vibrancy of the LGBTQIA+ community and welcomes people of all sexual orientations, gender identities, and expressions.

Who We Are

At Mild Mood Therapy, you are not just a diagnosis or a checklist. You are a whole person with a story, identities, and experiences that deserve care and respect.

The practice offers LGBTQIA+ affirming, culturally aware therapy that holds space for the intersections of race, culture, gender, sexuality, trauma, and systems. Together, we explore what it looks like to move toward self-acceptance, empowerment, and authenticity on your own terms.

Whether you are navigating coming out, exploring gender, working through relationship stress, or simply wanting a place to land and exhale, this is a space where you can bring your full self.

Bianca Carver, MS, LMHC

Licensed Mental Health Counselor

Bianca works primarily with adults navigating anxiety, depression, Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD), sexuality, trauma, and racial and cultural identity concerns. She has a special interest and deep experience supporting the LGBTQIA+ community.

Her approach blends Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Solution-Focused Therapy (SFT), Motivational Interviewing, and a warm, collaborative style. Sessions are structured enough to feel held and flexible enough to meet you exactly where you are that day.

Mission

Mild Mood Therapy exists to create a space where love is expansive and every person is honored for who they are. You are invited to come as you are and explore what healing, growth, and wholeness can look like when you do not have to shrink or perform.

The mission is to walk alongside you as you reconnect with yourself, build tools that actually fit your life, and move toward a version of you that feels more grounded, authentic, and emotionally resourced.
